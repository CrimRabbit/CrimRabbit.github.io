Rhyming name
Sun 12 March 2017 Writing blurbs

As a child growing up, I loved my name. It was rare, and my parents told me it was the flower that they grew up liking. Imagine my surprise when I found out that Carmellias was used in a whole bunch of stuff too! It is used for tea, it is used to care for your hair, it is even used to protect our kitchen knives!

The confusing thing is when my mother gave birth to twins after I was born, they were named Camilia and Emilia. I had thought our names were even more special – all of us siblings had names ending with -lia. We could be the lia siblings! But one day a distant uncle came to us, confused.

“So the tall one is Camilia, the long haired one is Emilia and the wife is Camilia? Eddard I thought your wife was Cornelia?”

Father wasn’t so amused that day.

- Joe Ng
