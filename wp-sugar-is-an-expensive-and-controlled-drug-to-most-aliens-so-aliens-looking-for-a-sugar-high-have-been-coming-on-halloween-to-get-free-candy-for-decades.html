[WP] Sugar is an expensive and controlled drug to most aliens. So aliens looking for a sugar high have been coming on Halloween to get free candy for decades.
Sat 11 March 2017 Writing blurbs

“Trick of treatings, hooman! We come with punpkeen carvings!” a tiny squeaking voice called.

I raised an eyebrow. Odd halloween greeting aside, on my porch stood two figures much shorter than the average human child. The one on the left wore a huge blue-petalled flower on her? head, with some water sloshing in the middle. With a very convincing turtle shell on the back and even webbed fingers and feet, a very convincing impression of a kappa look up at me. The other, thrusting out its oversized pumpkin carving pail, was holding a smile behind a pair of red goggles and an avaitor’s hat completed by a tiny rotor above it.

“Meep pa pere por pe pandies!” the blue one bounced in place, before receiving a quick jab by the other. “Not of the poobah speech, hooman is before us!”

Without being critial of their speech, this odd pair have indeed put much effort into their getup. The blue one has yet to spill any of the water from the flower on its head too. With the candy bowl on the counter still brimming with candies, I took more than a handful of candy and dropped them into their pumpkin carvings. Imagine my surprise when the carvings themselves seemed to smile after being filled up. The duo though, were even more estastic.

“Much candy is of the givings! Many thankings!” “Meep pre peep preeeep!”

Confused, I watched in amusement as they practically celebrated, radiating with cheer. Thrusting at my feet a tiny pumpkin carving, they gave me a grin so wide you would think they were not human to begin with. Before I could muster a response, they skipped off into the night waving at a speed too fast for someone in a costume. The pumpkin carving at my feet though somehow turned to face me, eyes seemingly looking straight through me.

“Hello hooman, I is here to bring you good tidings!”

- Joe Ng
